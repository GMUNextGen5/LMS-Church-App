4. Data Security & FERPA Compliance

We employ enterprise-grade security measures to protect your educational records. Although as a religious organization we may not be strictly bound by federal regulations, we voluntarily align our practices with the Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act (FERPA) to ensure the highest standard of privacy.

4.1 Security Measures

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted at rest using AES-256 encryption standards (the same standard used by banks). Data in transit is secured via TLS 1.3/HTTPS protocols.
  • Strict Access Control: We utilize granular Role-Based Access Control (RBAC).
    • Students can only access their own records.
    • Parents can only access their own children's records.
    • Teachers are restricted to accessing only the students assigned to their specific courses.
  • Advanced Protection: Our system includes active XSS (Cross-Site Scripting) prevention using DOMPurify sanitization and undergoes regular security audits.

4.2 FERPA Alignment

In alignment with FERPA principles, we guarantee:

  • The right for parents and eligible students to inspect and review their education records.
  • The right to request corrections to records believed to be inaccurate.
  • The assurance that personally identifiable information (PII) is not disclosed to third parties without consent, except as allowed by law for educational purposes.

5. AI & Automated Processing

Our System employs Artificial Intelligence (AI) to provide performance summaries, study tips, and administrative assistance. Data processed by AI is used solely for educational enhancement and is not sold to third parties for marketing. Please be aware that model-assisted content (such as study tips) is advisory in nature and should be verified.

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact the Administration.
